5. Броеви - Cardinal numbers 1-100 with gender agreement for 1 and 2
6. Глаголот "има" - "To have" conjugation (јас имам, ти имаш, etc.)
7. Показни заменки - Demonstratives (овој/оваа/ова, тој/таа/тоа, оној/онаа/она)
8. Придавки - Adjective agreement with nouns (gender, number, definiteness)
9. Именки - Noun genders (masculine -о/-е consonant, feminine -а/-ја, neuter -о/-е)
10. Заменки - Personal pronouns (јас, ти, тој, таа, тоа, ние, вие, тие)

Use Macedonian examples from actual lesson content where possible. Examples should be simple A1-level sentences.

Export as A1_GRAMMAR_CONTENT constant for use in parser.
