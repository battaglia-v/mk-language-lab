**Known issue discovered during planning:**
Verbs like "смееш" (you laugh) and "правиш" (you do) are tagged as `partOfSpeech: "noun"` when they're clearly verbs. Macedonian verb morphology patterns:
- 1st person singular: -ам, -ам, -ум (јадам, пијам, сакам)
- 2nd person singular: -ш (јадеш, пиеш, сакаш)
- 3rd person singular: -и, -е, -а (јади, пие, сака)

- First-appearance deduplication
