# Privacy Policy - MK Language Lab

Effective date: January 12, 2026

MK Language Lab ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use the MK Language Lab web app and Trusted Web Activity (TWA) app (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address
- Name (if provided)
- Profile image (if provided)
- Password (stored as a secure hash)

### Learning and Usage Data
- Lesson progress, XP, streaks, quiz results, and practice history
- Saved words, phrases, and reading/translation history
- App settings and preferences (stored locally on your device)

### Device and Log Data
- IP address, timestamps, and basic device/browser information
- Error and crash logs (when enabled)

### Content You Provide
- Text submitted for translation or analysis
- Feedback messages you send through the app

### Push Notification Data (if enabled)
- Push token and delivery metadata (used to send reminders)

## How We Use Information
- Provide and improve the learning experience
- Personalize lessons and track progress
- Maintain security and prevent abuse
- Monitor performance and fix errors
- Respond to support requests

## Third-Party Services
We use the following service providers to operate the App:
- Vercel Analytics (privacy-friendly usage analytics)
- Sentry (error monitoring and diagnostics)
- Google Cloud Translation API (translation features)
- Google Cloud Text-to-Speech (audio pronunciation, if enabled)
- Upstash (rate limiting and abuse protection)
- Database hosting (PostgreSQL)

These providers process data only on our behalf and under our instructions.

## No Sale or Marketing Sharing
We do not sell your personal information and do not share it with third parties for their own marketing or advertising purposes. We only share data with service providers as described above or when legally required.

## Data Retention
We retain account and learning data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us. We will delete your data within 30 days, subject to legal or security requirements.

## Your Choices
- Update or delete your account information
- Disable notifications in your device settings
- Clear local data stored by the App (localStorage/cache)

## Children's Privacy
MK Language Lab is safe for general audiences but is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will remove it.

## International Users
Your information may be processed in countries where we or our service providers operate, including the United States. We use appropriate safeguards to protect your data.

## Security
We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the effective date above when changes are made.
