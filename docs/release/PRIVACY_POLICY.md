## Introduction

Welcome to Македонски • MK Language Lab ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe, transparent learning experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application (the "App") and related services.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use the App.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:
- **Email address** – Used for authentication, account recovery, and important service notifications
- **Name** – Used for personalization (e.g., "Welcome back, [Name]!")
- **Password** – Securely hashed and stored; we never store passwords in plain text
- **OAuth provider details** – If you sign in via Google, Facebook, or other OAuth providers, we receive basic profile information (name, email, profile picture) from those services

### 2. Learning Progress Data

To provide personalized learning experiences, we collect:
- **Practice completions** – Which exercises you've completed and your accuracy
- **Mission progress** – Daily checklist items, XP earned, streak tracking
- **Quiz scores** – Performance on vocabulary, grammar, and listening exercises
- **Audio playback history** – Which audio clips you've played (for offline caching)
- **Translation history** – Text you've translated and the results (stored locally on your device)
- **Badge and quest achievements** – Milestones you've reached

### 3. Device and Usage Data

To improve app functionality and diagnose issues, we collect:
- **Device identifiers** – Expo push token (for notifications), device model, OS version
- **App usage statistics** – Which features you use, session duration, navigation patterns
- **Crash logs and diagnostics** – Error reports to identify and fix bugs
- **Locale and timezone** – To schedule reminders at appropriate times for your region

### 4. Optional Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:
- **Push notifications** – To send mission reminders and streak nudges (opt-in, can be disabled anytime)
- **Background app refresh** – To cache practice audio and sync progress while the app is closed (iOS only)
- **Network access** – Required for fetching content, submitting practice completions, and syncing data

## How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We only share your information in the following limited circumstances:

### 1. Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party service providers to operate the App:

**NextAuth (Authentication)**
- Purpose: OAuth-based authentication (Google, Facebook, etc.)
- Data shared: Email, name, profile picture (from OAuth provider)
- Privacy: NextAuth does not store your credentials; we manage session tokens securely
- Learn more: [https://next-auth.js.org/](https://next-auth.js.org/)

**Expo Push Notifications**
- Purpose: Deliver push notifications for mission reminders
- Data shared: Expo push token (device identifier), locale, timezone, reminder preferences
- Privacy: Tokens are stored securely and can be revoked by disabling notifications
- Learn more: [https://docs.expo.dev/push-notifications/overview/](https://docs.expo.dev/push-notifications/overview/)

**Vercel (Hosting & Analytics)**
- Purpose: Host the backend API and collect privacy-friendly analytics
- Data shared: Anonymous usage statistics (page views, feature interactions)
- Privacy: No cookies, no personally identifiable information (PII), GDPR compliant
- Learn more: [https://vercel.com/docs/analytics](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics)

**Sentry (Error Tracking)**
- Purpose: Monitor crashes and errors to improve app stability
- Data shared: Crash logs, stack traces, device model, OS version (no PII)
- Privacy: We sanitize logs to remove sensitive information before submission
- Learn more: [https://sentry.io/privacy/](https://sentry.io/privacy/)

### 2. Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, such as:
- To comply with a subpoena, court order, or legal process
- To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public
- To enforce our Terms of Service or investigate potential violations

### 3. Business Transfers

If we are acquired by or merge with another company, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email or prominent notice in the App before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures

We take data security seriously and implement industry-standard measures:
- **Encryption in transit:** All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- **Encryption at rest:** User data stored in our database is encrypted at rest
- **Secure storage:** On your device, sensitive data (session tokens, push tokens) is stored using iOS Keychain (iOS) or Android Keychain (Android)
- **Access controls:** Only authorized personnel can access user data, and access is logged and monitored
- **Regular security audits:** We periodically review our security practices and update them as needed

### Data Retention

- **Account data:** Retained as long as your account is active
- **Learning progress:** Retained indefinitely while your account is active (to track long-term progress)
- **Crash logs:** Retained for 90 days after collection
- **Analytics data:** Aggregated and anonymized analytics retained indefinitely for trend analysis

### Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by:
1. Emailing us at **support@makedonski.mk** with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"
2. Providing your registered email address for verification

We will delete your account and all associated data within **30 days** of receiving your request. Note that some data may be retained in anonymized form for analytics purposes or as required by law.

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights under GDPR:
