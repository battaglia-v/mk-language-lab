## Description (4000 chars max)

Master the Macedonian language with Македонски • MK Language Lab – your complete learning companion powered by AI and real-world content.

**DAILY MISSIONS & SMART PRACTICE**
Start your day with personalized learning missions featuring:
• Interactive checklist tracking your progress across vocabulary, grammar, and culture
• Coach tips tailored to your learning style
• Smart Review system that surfaces words you need to practice most
• Quick Practice sessions with swipeable cards: cloze exercises, multiple choice, listening comprehension, and typing challenges
• Hearts & XP system with streak tracking to keep you motivated
• Audio pronunciation for every exercise with offline caching

**INSTANT TRANSLATION**
Break through language barriers:
• Macedonian ↔ English translation with automatic language detection
• History sync across all features so you can revisit past translations
• Copy translations instantly or save them to your mission progress
• Works online and caches results for offline review

**DISCOVER AUTHENTIC CONTENT**
Immerse yourself in real Macedonian:
• Curated editorial feed with category filters (Culture, Grammar, Vocabulary, Phrases)
• Live news headlines from Macedonian sources
• Upcoming Word Lab events and community learning sessions
• Articles and resources hand-picked for language learners

**SMART REMINDERS**
Never break your streak:
• Customizable push notifications for morning, lunch, or evening practice
• Mission deadline reminders that adapt to your timezone
• Background sync keeps your progress current even when offline

**PROFILE & PROGRESS**
Track your journey:
• Real-time XP, level, and streak statistics
• Quest milestones with badges (Vocabulary Master, Grammar Guru, Culture Explorer)
• Detailed learning analytics showing your strengths and areas to improve

**OFFLINE-FIRST DESIGN**
Learn anywhere, anytime:
• Practice decks and audio cache automatically for offline use
• Mission data syncs when you reconnect
• Completion queue replays your XP when network returns

**PRIVACY-FRIENDLY**
We respect your data:
• No ads, no third-party tracking beyond essential analytics
• Your translations and practice history stay on your device
• Push notifications are opt-in and fully configurable

Whether you're planning a trip to Macedonia, connecting with family, or exploring Balkan languages, Македонски • MK Language Lab makes learning Macedonian engaging, efficient, and fun.

Download now and start your first mission today!

**ABOUT THIS APP**
Built by language learners, for language learners. Македонски • MK Language Lab combines modern mobile UX with proven spaced-repetition techniques and authentic Macedonian content. Join our growing community and discover how enjoyable language learning can be.

Questions or feedback? Contact us at support@makedonski.mk or visit our website at https://makedonski.mk
